Bullet friends

How to play

Move with arrow keys or WASD

Dash with Space when meter is full. The start of your dash is invulnerable

Avoid the bullets (red ... things)

Collect 10 candies (yellow circles) to open the portals (green circles)

Enter the portals (green circles) to go to the next wave

Going to the next wave cashes in your candies for points

candy is worth more in later waves
